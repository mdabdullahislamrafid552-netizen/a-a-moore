How is AI priced?+

A setup fee plus a monthly operations fee. Setup is based on integration count and customization; monthly cost reflects usage volume and ongoing tuning.

When does AI not make sense?+

When the volume isn't there, the workflow is genuinely high-judgment, or the brand experience would suffer. We'll tell you before we sell you.

What about data privacy?+

We architect against your privacy and compliance requirements from day one. HIPAA-ready and SOC 2-aligned deployments available.
