Common questions

Things people ask
before signing.

How long does it take?+

Most full plans run 6 – 10 weeks depending on scope and how quickly your team can turn around interviews and data. We can compress if there's a real deadline.

What sets the project price?+

Depth of primary research, complexity of the financial model, number of business lines and whether the plan is investor-facing. We quote a fixed fee at scoping.

Can I start with hourly first?+

Yes — many engagements start with a few hourly sessions to clarify the question before committing to a full project.
