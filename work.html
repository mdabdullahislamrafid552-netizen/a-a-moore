Featured engagement · 2025
Client

Halsted Mfg.

Sector

Industrial / B2B

Services

Business Plan · Sales Strategy · Brand

Timeline

14 weeks · 2024–25

A second-generation manufacturer had hit a wall: orders were steady, margins were not, and the leadership team couldn't agree on whether the problem was sales, operations, or simply a market that had moved on without them.

We spent the first four weeks doing what most engagements skip: listening. To shop-floor supervisors, to the controller, to the two largest customers, and to the founder who'd been running the place since 1998. The diagnosis that emerged wasn't the one anyone expected — the sales process was fine, but the company was quoting work it shouldn't have been bidding on at all.

"Half the work we won was work that made us money on paper and lost us money in practice. Once we stopped chasing it, the rest of the business got easier overnight."

From there the work split into three streams: a rebuilt pricing and qualification model, a three-year operating plan with the leadership team co-authoring it section by section, and a brand refresh tied to the new positioning. Twelve months later, revenue is up 62%, the team is smaller, and the founder has Saturdays back.
