Common questions

Things people ask
before signing.

Do you offer commission-only engagements?+

No. We build infrastructure, design strategies, and active-coach sales reps which requires significant upfront and ongoing time investment. We structure our fees around fixed projects or retainers, though performance bonuses can be discussed for long-term engagements.

What industries do you specialize in?+

We work primarily with B2B services, SaaS, high-ticket consulting, and professional construction/industrial firms. If your customer requires a consultative sales process with a deal size over $5k, our models are optimized for you.

Can we transition to hourly later?+

Yes. Once your pipeline is established and your internal team is running it, many clients transition to our monthly retainer or strategic hourly reviews to ensure they stay on track.
