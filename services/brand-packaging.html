Common questions

Things people ask
before signing.

What determines the price?+

Scope of work primarily — a brand refresh and a full brand build are very different engagements. Strategy depth, number of applications, and whether naming is involved are the next biggest drivers.

Do you offer naming as a standalone?+

Yes — naming alone is a smaller scoped engagement. Often easier to bundle with a brand build, but it's available either way.

How many rounds of revision?+

Two structured revision rounds per major deliverable. Additional rounds are quoted before any extra work begins.
