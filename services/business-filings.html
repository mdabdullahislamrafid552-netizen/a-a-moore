Common questions

Things people ask
before signing.

Are you an attorney or law firm?+

A&A Moore is a business consultancy — we file business documents that don't require an attorney signature, and we partner with licensed attorneys for anything that does.

What counts as a "complex" filing?+

Anything multi-state, custom operating agreements, restructures, or filings requiring meaningful additional research or drafting beyond a standard 3-page document.

What about ongoing compliance?+

Annual report filings, registered agent service and compliance calendar can be added as a recurring service after the initial setup.
