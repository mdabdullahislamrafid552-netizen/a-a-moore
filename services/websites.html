Common questions

Things people ask
before signing.

What determines the price?+

Number of unique page templates, the platform we're building on, integrations involved, and whether there's any custom backend work. We quote a fixed fee at scoping.

What platform do you build on?+

Webflow and Framer for most marketing sites. Next.js or Astro for sites with real backend requirements. We pick the right tool for the job.

Can our team edit the site after launch?+

Yes — that's the point. CMS training is included so your team can update content without coming back to us.
